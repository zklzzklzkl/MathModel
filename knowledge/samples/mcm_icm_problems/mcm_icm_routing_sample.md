# MCM/ICM Problem Routing Sample

MCM/ICM problems often hide multiple deliverables in narrative wording: a model, a policy recommendation, sensitivity analysis, and an executive-style explanation.

Typical route signals:

- "Develop a model" usually requires an interpretable formulation, not only prediction accuracy.
- "Evaluate" asks for criteria, normalization, weighting, and ranking robustness.
- "Recommend" asks for a decision rule, constraints, trade-offs, and failure cases.
- "Communicate to stakeholders" requires clear figures and non-technical wording.

Recommended use: during intake, extract action verbs and deliverables in English, then map them to mixed problem types for the model router.

Risk warning: avoid translating an English prompt into a Chinese template too early; preserve the original action verbs and constraints.
