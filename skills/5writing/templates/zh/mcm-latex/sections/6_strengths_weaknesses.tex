\section{Strengths and Weaknesses}

\subsection{Strengths}

\begin{enumerate}
  \item Solid theoretical foundations with rigorous derivations.
  \item Multi-model comparison ensures optimal selection.
  \item High accuracy ($R^2 = 0.923$) with good interpretability.
  \item Computationally efficient for large-scale applications.
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Weaknesses}

\begin{enumerate}
  \item Assumes data stationarity; may not hold for rapidly changing systems.
  \item Does not capture temporal dynamics explicitly.
\end{enumerate}
