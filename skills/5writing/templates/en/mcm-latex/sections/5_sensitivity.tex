\section{Sensitivity Analysis}

We perform sensitivity analysis by varying key parameters within reasonable ranges to assess their impact on the model outputs and robustness.

\subsection{Parameter Variation}
We vary the regularization parameter $\lambda$ and observe its effect on the test $R^2$ score.

\subsection{Results}
The model is relatively insensitive to $\lambda$ in the range $[0.01, 0.5]$, with $R^2$ changing by less than 1\%.
