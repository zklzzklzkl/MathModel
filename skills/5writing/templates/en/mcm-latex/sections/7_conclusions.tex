\section{Conclusions}

This paper presents a comprehensive approach to the data-driven optimization problem.
By combining multiple modeling techniques and rigorous validation, we developed a robust
solution with strong predictive performance.

The Random Forest model achieved a test $R^2$ of 0.896, and the optimized ensemble
reached $R^2 = 0.923$ with a 15\% reduction in RMSE compared to the baseline.
The genetic algorithm provided an efficient strategy for constrained optimization.

Future work could explore deep learning approaches for larger datasets and
Bayesian optimization for hyperparameter tuning.
